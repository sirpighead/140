Learn about the conflict in Ukraine

Thousands are dying and millions are fleeing.

The war in Ukraine is still worsening. As of March 21st, over 3.5 million people have fled the country as refugees. Hundreds of thousands more have left their homes and are stranded elsewhere in the country. See more news and statistics here.

This crisis is not a new one.

The tensions between Russia and Ukraine date all the way to before even World War II. However, most point to Russia's seizing of Crimea in 2014 as the start of the modern war in Ukraine. The aggressor country had also been stoking the flames of rebellion in Ukraine's Donbas region for years, leading to the pro-Russian parties in Donetsk and Luhansk to declare independece in 2014. Read more about the history of the conflict in Ukraine here.

Ukraine needs our help.

Many fundraising campaigns have taken off in support of the people in Ukraine. With an insane number needing to flee their homes, the refugees in the war-torn country need all the help they can get. Learn more about what you can do to help at the support page.
