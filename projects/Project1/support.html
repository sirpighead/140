Relief Funds - help the people of Ukraine

UNICEF

Donate

UNICEF is an organization that specifically helps the children affected by conflict all over the world. Right now they are working in Ukraine to provide safe water, nutrition, education, and medical care to children and their families who have been displaced by the conflict.

Ukraine Red Cross

Donate

The Ukraine Red Cross has been on the front lines of this extended conflict since day one. They mobilize volunteers whoe provide emergency medical aid to people affected by war in Ukraine.

Ukraine Media Fighting Against Propaganda

Donate

Take a look at this fundraiser organized by Jakub Parusinski, who is fighting a war to keep the people of Ukraine informed. The plague of Russian propaganda is detrimental to the Ukrainian's fight for freedom, and Jakub has been working tirelessly to counteract it.

World Central Kitchen

Donate

The World Central Kitchen sends trained chefs to the front lines of crises to help feed anyone affected. They provide a sense of security in the form of hot meals for refugees.

UN Refugee Agency

Donate

The UNHCR provides emergency supplies to refugees of conflicts all over the world. This relief fund specifically targets the refugees fleeing Ukraine, providing them with much needed goods like tents, clothes, and medical supplies.

International Rescue Committee

Donate

The IRC helps refugees and IDPs in countries afflicted by war. They are operating in over 40 countries right now, including Ukraine. By providing necessities that refugees don't have access to right now, due to dangerous and unstable infrastructure, the IRC is actively saving lives of those upended by the crisis.
